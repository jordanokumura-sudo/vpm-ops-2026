On the Floor: Shared Narrative

Everyone on the team should be able to say this in their own words:

"If someone were to Google you, what would they find? We help companies actively build their digital presence and reputation—a layered, cumulative digital mosaic that proves who they are, what they've built, and why they matter. If companies don't shape this narrative themselves, it will be shaped for them—by algorithms, competitors, AI summaries, and search results that flatten nuance and value."
OPS-specific thread

Organic as identity. How are companies shaping their organic story beyond the certification? Where does the premium hold, and where is it being competed away? Listen closely—this is the editorial thread we'll be pulling through H2.

One narrative, shared across every conversation. Inconsistency is the failure mode.

Questions We're Asking

  • Where do you see OPS / the organic category heading in the next few years?
  • What's feeling most different in the current market or show landscape?
  • What victories have you celebrated in the past year?
  • Where are you feeling the most pressure right now?
  • Where can we better support you?
  • Where could stronger storytelling, visibility, or marketing make a difference for you?
Always ask before leaving a booth "What are the top two or three topics impacting you—or the industry at large—that you'd love to read more about?" Treat the answer as editorial-pipeline gold. It's next quarter's story calendar, sourced straight from the floor.
