On the Floor: Shared Narrative

Everyone on the team should be able to say this in their own words:

"If someone were to Google you, what would they find? We help companies actively build their digital presence and reputation—a layered, cumulative digital mosaic that proves who they are, what they've built, and why they matter."

OPS thread: Organic as identity. How are companies shaping their story beyond the certification? Where does the premium hold, and where is it being competed away?

Questions We're Asking

  • Where do you see OPS / the organic category heading in the next few years?
  • What's feeling most different in the current market or show landscape?
  • What victories have you celebrated in the past year?
  • Where are you feeling the most pressure right now?
  • Where can we better support you?
  • Where could stronger storytelling, visibility, or marketing make a difference for you?
Always ask before leaving a booth"What are the top two or three topics impacting you—or the industry at large—that you'd love to read more about?"
